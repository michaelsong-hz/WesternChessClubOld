CUCC 2017 Information

This year, the Canadian University Chess Championships (CUCC) is coming to Western University. This annual tournament features the best university chess teams across Canada locked in a battle to outwit their opponent, lay claim to first place and subsequently, have their names engraved on the Queen's Cup.

Eligibility

  • All tournament members must be university students or staff members
  • All tournament members must also be members of The Chess Federation of Canada (non-current members can register at the event)

Sections

  • Open: Any team may register in this section
  • Reserves: Average team rating must be below 1800 CFC

Tournament Format

  • 5 rounds of Team Swiss (Teams of 4 players)
  • Teams are allowed but not required to have a substitute fifth player on their team
  • Teams can substitute the fifth player as they please
  • The board order must be locked before round 1.
  • Team formation should respect the order of strength.

Time Control

  • 75 min + 30 sec per move for ALL rounds
