CUCC 2017 Information

This year, the Canadian University Chess Championships (CUCC) is coming to The University of Western Ontario (also known as Western University). This annual tournament features the best university chess teams across Canada locked in a battle to outwit their opponent, lay claim to first place and subsequently, have their names engraved on the Queen's Cup.

If you are a Western University student, please do not register here. Contact us to find out how you can join one of our CUCC teams.

Eligibility

  • All tournament members must be university students or staff members
  • All tournament members must also be members of The Chess Federation of Canada (non-current members may register at the event)

Sections

  • Open: Any team may register in this section
  • Reserves: Average team rating must be below 1800 CFC

Tournament Format

  • 5 rounds of Team Swiss (Teams of 4 players)
  • Teams are allowed but not required to have a substitute fifth player on their team
  • Teams may substitute the fifth player as they please
  • The board order must be locked before round 1
  • Team formation should respect the order of strength

Time Control

  • 75 min + 30 sec per move for ALL rounds
